FAQs

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our solar solutions at HARVESTERKEN.

What are the advantages of choosing solar energy?
Solar energy offers numerous benefits, including lower energy bills, a smaller environmental footprint, enhanced energy independence, and potential financial incentives like tax benefits. Our solar solutions are clean, green, and tailored to your specific needs.
How does solar panel installation work?
Our expert team designs and installs your solar system efficiently. We handle site assessment, panel placement, electrical integration, and system testing to ensure maximum energy efficiency.
What's the typical payback period for a HARVESTERKEN system?
Most residential systems have a payback period of 4-7 years, depending on energy consumption and government incentives.
What maintenance is required for solar panels?
Minimal maintenance is required. Regular cleaning and occasional inspection ensure optimal performance. HARVESTERKEN also offers unlimited maintenance for select plans.
How long is the PPA for a WiSolar prepaid system?
The Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) typically lasts 24 months, providing a predictable cost structure for your solar energy.
Can I own the system before the end of the PPA?
Yes, HARVESTERKEN allows early ownership options. Contact us to discuss terms and options for early system transfer.
